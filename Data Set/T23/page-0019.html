Title: User Experience Architect
Company: Model Metrics
Job Code: MM-UX
Location(s): Illinois - CHICAGO
Industries:
Advertising
Functions:
Consulting
Information Technology
Strategy - Planning
Job Type: Fulltime
Compensation:
Description
We are noted as one of the fastest growing companies in Chicago with 80 percent year over year revenue growth and 75 percent employee growth. We are Model Metrics and have just been ranked the #1 Top Workplace for Employees in our Category by the Chicago Tribune; we have also been named as a finalist in the 2010 Chicago Innovation Awards for its 2GO Mobile Cloud Platform. We were also named a Gartner 2011 Cool Vendor in Application Services and a leader in the Forrester Wave for Salesforce.com SI’s for companies under $100M.

Model Metrics is an industry pioneering consulting firm, helping enterprise organizations accelerate the adoption of Cloud Computing. Model Metrics has forged relationships with the nation’s leading Cloud Computing companies, including salesforce.com, Amazon, Apple, and Google. No more servers. No more software. No more upgrade headaches. Just tremendous ROI! Headquartered in Chicago, the Model Metrics team is highly respected in taking bleeding edge technologies and helping businesses understand how to leverage them successfully, from custom mobile solutions for iPad and Android built in the Cloud, to strategic business process work to change management and adoption.

As our User Experience Architect, you will be responsible for the review of all creative elements associated with our most important client deliverables across all types of projects, including customer and partner portals, social applications for business, mobile applications on iPads, Android tablets, smartphones and enterprise business applications across nearly every department function. You will work closely with our Delivery team, assign internal and external resources for projects, and use a sharp eye to ensure adherence to quality guidelines. You must have relevant experience creating and managing design direction for clients within various verticals. Your strong understanding of UX and UI will add to brainstorm new concepts and develop design mocks for clients. You will have the leadership ability to oversee and coach a creative team of Producers, Designers, and Developers.

In order to qualify you must have the following essential skills:

• Bachelor’s degree required from an accredited 4 year university or college or equivalent experience

• At least 5 years in professional services or agency experience

• A strong background in HCI or interactive design

• Experience with prototyping and wireframing tools (Balsamiq, OmniGraffle, Visio) and techniques

• Experience interacting with technical teams

• Strong attention to detail and passion for creative thinking

• Experience managing a creative team

• Track record providing quality control over concepts and projects

• Strong project management skills

• Ability to Interact with high-level clients and demonstrate strategic understanding of business needs

• Ability to multi-task in a fast paced environment

• Willingness travel up to 50%

Preferred skills would include:

• Experience in working with advertising or design agencies

• Familiarity with client side technology (css, html, javascript) and design related concerns accompanying those technologies

• Strong knowledge of Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Flash, and Adobe Flex/AIR

• A thorough understanding of web, desktop and mobile design, social marketing media, and cloud industry


Model Metrics encourages a culture that is creative, innovative and highly collaborative. We offer a competitive salary and benefits package, opportunity for continuous education/certifications, flexible hours, tools for working remotely, employer subsidized mobile phone and wireless card, casual dress at the office, fully subsidized beverage and snack center and a green office. Model Metrics continues to give back to the community through our Model Citizen corporate giving initiative. We encourage you to apply to learn more.
