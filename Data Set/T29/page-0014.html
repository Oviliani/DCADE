Beverly Hills Chihuahua

Rating: PG Length: 93 mins. Year: 2008
Cast: Voices by Drew Barrymore, Salma Hayek, Jamie Lee Curtis, Andy Garcia, George Lopez

Buy on Blu-Ray

Movie Description
Pampered pooch Chloe spends her days enjoying the comforts of her luxurious lifestyle to the fullest. When a vacation to Mexico leaves her far from her palatial pleasures, she sees a side of the world she never knew existed. Befriending street inhabiters Delgado and Papi, she discovers it might be possible to survive without a spa and pedicure!
