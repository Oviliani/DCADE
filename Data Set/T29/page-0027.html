Princess and the Frog, The

Rating: G Length: 97 mins. Year: 2009
Cast: Terence Howard, Oprah Winfrey, Anika Noni Rose

Share46

Movie Description
A classic tale is brought to life with hand-drawn animation from Walt Disney Studios. Cursed by a witch doctor, Prince Naveen implores the beautiful Princess Tiana for one kiss. If a princess kissing a frog works in fairytales, surely it will work now, right? Join the creators of "Aladdin" and "The Little Mermaid" on this downright funny and exciting journey though the French Quarter. The famous voices of Anika Noni Rose, John Goodman, and Oprah Winfrey make this new spin on a classic tale all the more delightful.
