Ponyo

Rating: G Length: 103 mins. Year: 2009
Cast: Voices by Cate Blanchett, Noah Cyrus, Matt Damon, Tina Fey

Movie Description
Walt Disney Studios teams up with award winning director Hayao Miyazaki in this splashing tale inspired by Hans Christian Andersen's "The Little Mermaid" and traditional Japanese folklore. 5 year-old Sosuke (voiced by Frankie Jonas) befriends an all too eager goldfish princess named Ponyo (voiced by Noah Cyrus), who longs for nothing more than to be human. This film also has an amazing cast of voice talent with Cate Blanchett, Matt Damon, Tina Fey, Cloris Leachman, Lily Tomlin, and Betty White.
