Full details

Property reference: HRT106203518

A beautifully designed detached house situated in Hollacombe Lane, Paignton giving easy access to both Paignton & Torquay. The accommodation comprises of entrance hall, downstairs w.c, living room, dining room, modern kitchen, utility, five/six bedrooms, two en-suite's and a family bathroom. The property has many benefits such as a gym, sea views, an ideal location, gardens, a garage and parking. Viewing recommended.
