Full details

Property reference: HRT019304169

This large period home has been extended to provide wonderful family accommodation over 3 floors. This lovely home retains many original features including doors, box sash replacement double glazed windows and gas heating. The accommodation provides bright double aspect reception/dining room, modern extended kitchen/family room which leads onto the west facing garden, 3 excellent double bedrooms and updated family bathroom to first floor and a bright 'L' shaped Master bedroom and bathroom to top floor. The garden extends in excess of 55’ and is west facing with mature trees and shrubs - ideal for family needs and relaxing. Oxford Avenue is ideally located for excellent schools, Wimbledon Centre and many local amenities. A lovely family home not to be missed.


Features:

Entrance

Lovely period style front door to hall with coved ceiling, halogen lighting, dado rail, large boxed in radiator, stairs with original newel post to upper floors, understairs storage, wood effect flooring.

Reception Room

Bright double aspect reception room with feature period style fireplace with cast iron fireplace and coloured slips, mantlepiece surround, original coved ceiling, boxed sash double glazed window overlooking front, double radiator, window overlooking garden, original door, wood laminated floor, great reception room.

Kitchen/Family Room

Wonderful Kitchen/Family Room leading onto the west facing garden with period style floor and wall units in light wood, wood granite effect work surfaces twin bowl stainless sink inset to work surface, 5 ring Neff hob inset with stainless steel built in double oven, space for large fridge/freezer, plumbing for domestic appliances, double doored cupboard housing gas combination boiler.

Landing

Built in airing cupboard, dado rail, radiator, halogen lighting, original newel post and spindles and stairs top floor.

Bedroom 2

Large double bedroom with box sash double glazed twin sash window to front, 3rd box sash double glazed sash window, coved ceiling, double radiator, original door.

Bedroom 3

Another excellent double bedroom overlooking garden, original style sash window, radiator, original door.

Bedroom 4

Original style sash window overlooking garden, radiator, access to rear loft space, great double bedroom, original door.

Bathroom

2 Obscure glazed windows, modern 3 piece suite of tiled panelled bath, modern wall fittings, shower screen, wall mounted wash hand basin with mixer tap, ladder radiator, wood flooring.

Top Floor Landing

Domed skylight, dado rail.

Bathroom

Panelled bath with wall mounted mixer tap and shower controls, shower screen, pedestal wash hand basin with monobloc tap, low level W.C, 1/2 tiled and tiled walls with mosaic inset tiling, wood floor, halogen lighting, sash style, double glazed window, ladder radiator.

Master Bedroom

Lovely bright Master Bedroom with 2 double glazed sash style window overlooking rear, 3 large windows, eaves storage, range of built in wardrobes, light wood flooring, halogen lighting, double radiator.

Garden

Side gate to front, circular paved patio, idea for entertaining relaxing, lawn with shrub and border screening, trees, garden shed, decking to rear of garden, water supply, electric and lighting.

Tenure

Freehold.

DIRECTIONS:

Go under bridge and turn left at lights, continue along Kingston Road to next major lights and turn left. Take 1st left into Oxford Avenue. The property is on the left.
