Full details

Property reference: HRT021604249

An attractive brick fronted Victorian house thoughtfully extended and much improved by the current owners. The accommodation comprises on the ground floor front reception room open plan to the kitchen/reception room and cloakroom, on the first floor two double bedrooms, study and family bathroom; and on the second floor master bedroom with en suite shower room.


Features:

Entrance

Stripped and polished wooden floor boards, two under stairs cupboards.

Cloakroom

Window to rear, wash hand basin, low level W.C., chrome towel rail/radiator, plumbing for washing machine, tiled floor.

Reception Room One 14' x 13'2 (4.27m x 4.01m)

Square bay double glazed wooden framed sash windows to front, coving, display Niche to chimney breast, double radiator, stripped and polished wooden floor boards, square arch and open plan to kitchen/family room.

Kitchen/Reception Room 24' x 19'4 (7.32m x 5.89m)

(7.32m x 5.89m) Kitchen area - stainless steel single drainer sink unit, range of fitted wall and base units, stainless steel gas hob, electric oven, integrated dishwasher, fridge/freezer, tiled splash back, gas central heating combination boiler. Dining area / display niche to chimney breast, radiator, stripped and polished wooden floor boards leading to area with slate tiled floor, two Velux windows to ceiling, low voltage lights, double glazed wooden framed folding doors to garden.

Landing
