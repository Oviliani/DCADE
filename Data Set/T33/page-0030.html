SUTHERLAND GROVE SW18
£725,000

For Sale

Mid-Terrace House, 5 bedrooms, Freehold

A superb double fronted 5 bedroom semi detached family house with an impressive 24' kitchen/family room, wine cellar, 2 receptions and parking for 3 cars. Further loft potential (STPP), situated on the borders of Putney & Southfields.

Full details

Property reference: HRT020204537

Situated within close proximity to St Michael's & St Cecilia's Schools ideal for a large family, boasting larger than average ground floor living space with a 2 storey side addition to make the property double fronted. Master bedroom suite plus a good sized en-suite bathroom with a jacuzzi bath and a separate shower cubicle. . For the wine connessieurs there is a unique wine cellar to help entertain your friends. No chain, sole agents, viewing is highly recommended.


Features:

Front Garden

Walled front garden with hedgerow borders, lawn with pathway to front door. Brick tiled driveway with off street parking for 3 cars and potential for more if required.

Porch

Tiled floor, front door with leaded windows either side leading to:

Entrance Hall

Parquet flooring, attractive wooden ceiling beams and ornate wood architraves. Double radiator, understairs storage cupboards, thermostat control, stripped wooden banisters and stairs to first floor.

Living Room

Large bay to front with leaded windows, top lights and secondary glazing. Ceiling rose, moulded cornice, fireplace with slate hearth and gas point, dwarf cupboard and bookcase set into recess either side. TV aerial point, double radiator, telephone point, laminate flooring.

Dining Room

Feature fireplace with tiled inserts, moulded cornice, picture rail and ceiling rose, double glazed patio doors to rear decked garden, laminate flooring.

Scullery

Originally the kitchen, now with a range of 'Shaker' fitted wall and base units to match the new kitchen, tiled splashback, large understairs storage cupboard, double radiator, twin doors with leaded windows inset for good natural light, access to hallway and utility room. Arch leading sideways to:

Kitchen/Breakfast Room

Very impressive kitchen and family room comprising a comprehensive range of 'Shaker' fitted wall and base units with tiled splashback, double bowl sink unit inset to work surface, integrated dishwasher, 'Diplomat' stainless steel six burner range cooker with hood above, space for fridge/freezer. Breakfast bar leading through to: Family/breakfast area: With double glazed windows on three sides for good natural light and double glazed doors to garden. Recess ceiling spotlights, moulded cornice, picture rail, vinyl flooring, TV aerial point. Trap door to:

Wine Cellar

A unique 7ft deep circular and tanked wine cellar, which was specifically designed when the side addition was added, ideal for a wine enthusiast. 'Ships' style staircase for access with lighting and hexagonal shaped wine storage shelves. Sadly wine not included!

Utility Room

Matching 'Shaker' style base units with plumbing for washing machine, space for dryer, sink unit, double glazed window and an airing cupboard housing the megaflow hot water cylinder. Double glazed door for side access to the garden, vinyl flooring, door to:

Cloakroom

Low level wc, vinyl flooring, double glazed window.

First Floor Landing

Stripped wooden picture rail and ceiling beams, double radiator. Large skylight with access to loft, potential to convert into two further bedrooms and a bathroom if required, subject to obtaining usual planning permissions and consents. Part prepared on the new addition section when built.

Bedroom 1

Master bedroom suite with double glazed window to front and additional window to side, moulded cornice, ceiling rose, picture rail, double radiator. Range of triple built-in wardrobes, with central doors leading via arch to:

En-suite Bathroom

Modern white suite comprising jacuzzi bath, sink unit, low level wc, large fully tiled corner double shower with tiled splashback, shaver socket. Heated towel rail, moulded cornice, recess ceiling spotlight and double glazed window.

Bedroom 5/Study

Dual aspect double glazed windows, double radiator, recess ceiling spotlights, moulded cornice, telephone point. Currently arranged as a study with built-in bookcase and additional power points for any necessary computer equipment.

Bedroom 2

Bay to front with leaded windows and top lights, picture rail, moulded cornice, range of built-in wardrobes, radiator, telephone point. Currently painted with an attractive jungle scene, ideal for children.

Bedroom 3

Secondary glazed picture window, wall of built-in wardrobes, double radiator.

Bedroom 4

Double radiator, picture rail, leaded window with top lights, telephone point.

Family Bathroom

White suite comprising panelled bath with mixer tap and shower tap. Overhead built-in power shower, glass screen and tiled splashback. Concealed flush wc, wash basin set into marble top unit, moulded cornice, recess ceiling spotlights, shaver socket, double glazed window.

Outside

Rear and side gardens arranged as a good sized patio ideal for summer barbeques, with steps up to slightly raised lawned area with fenced borders. Following round to a large raised deck, ideal for alfresco dining with a secluded outlook plus slate and bark chipping flowerbeds. Gate for access to front garden.

Directions
