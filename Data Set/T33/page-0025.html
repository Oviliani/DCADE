Full details

Property reference: HRT019304056

The Period Conversion flat retains many fine original and enhanced features to include stripped wood original dresser in kitchen, original doors and fireplaces and lovely high ceiling throughout. The larger than average accommodation is deceptively spacious offering garden aspect reception room leading to verandah, updated great size kitchen, larger than average double bedroom and recently modernised quality bathroom. In addition the property benefits from double glazing, gas heating, with off road parking, garage and a 1/3rd share of Freehold. The communal gardens are well maintained and of great size with outside storage, ideal for all garden equipment and furniture.
