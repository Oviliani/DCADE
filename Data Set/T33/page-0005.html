Granville Road, Southfields, London, SW18

GRANVILLE ROAD SW18
£685,000

For Sale

Mid-Terrace House, 4 bedrooms, Freehold

A spacious 4/5 bedroom family house close to the popular St Michael's School on Granville Road. Benefits include 2 reception rooms, kitchen, conservatory, 49' south facing garden, off street parking & close to the tube.

Full details

Property reference: HRT020204563

A superb house for a family, which offers plenty of living space on the ground floor, especially the sunny conservatory which would be an ideal playroom if required. Three bedrooms and a decent sized family bathroom on the first floor, plus the large loft which has been converted into an impressive master bedroom with a luxury en-suite shower room, with the option to use the 5th bedroom as a study or a nursery. Located within easy reach of all local amenities and Southfields tube station.


Features:

Front Garden

Driveway with off street parking for one car, hedgerow borders, mature trees and shrubs. Porch and front door to:

Entrance Hall

Picture rail, radiator, parquet flooring, thermostat control, understairs storage cupboard.

Cloakroom

Low level w/c, wash basin and extractor fan.

Kitchen

Range of 'Limed Oak' wall and base units with working surfaces over, tiled splashback, one and a half bowl sink unit with mixer tap. Built-in oven, gas hob, extractor hood above, space for fridge/freezer and plumbing for washing machine.Wall mounted gas central heating boiler, tiled floor. Window and door to garden.

Double Reception Room

Lounge: Attractive feature fireplace with ornate surround and gas coal effect fire, marble hearth, picture rail, ornate ceiling cornice and centre rose. Double radiator, stripped wooden door, large bay window to front, parquet flooring, arch to: Dining Room: Attractive feature fireplace with gas coal effect fire, ornate surround and marble hearth, picture rail, ornate ceiling cornice and centre rose, parquet flooring, stripped wooden door, double radiators, french doors to:

Conservatory

Quality Cathedral style "Amdega" conservatory with wood double glazed windows and french doors to garden. Fitted blinds and marble tiled floor with underfloor heating.

First Floor Landing

Matching staircase to second floor.

Bedroom 2 (Front)

Range of fully fitted wardrobes, large bay window to front, radiator, picture rail, ceiling rose and stripped wooden door.

Bedroom 3 (Rear)

Comprehensive range of limed oak built-in wardrobes, south facing picture window, picture rail, ceiling rose, airing cupboard, radiator and stripped wooden door.

Bedroom 4 (Front)

Picture window, radiator, stripped wooden door.

Bathroom

A good sized family bathroom with a white suite comprising panelled bath with mixer tap and overhead Mira shower. Tiled splashback, glass shower screen, wash basin set into vanity unit, low level w/c, radiator, vinyl flooring, opaque picture window and original access hatch to loft, now closed.

2nd Floor Landing

Skylight for good natural light.

Bedroom 1

A spacious loft conversion with good head height, recessed ceiling spotlights, double radiator, built-in double wardrobe, recess with storage above. Double glazed french doors to Juliet balcony. Fire door for access to bedroom 5, attractive stained glass window and door to:

En-Suite Shower Room

Luxury walk-in shower with curved fossilised glass screen, power shower, marble tiled splashback, low level w/c. Unique stone sink set into granite work top, mirror fronted bathroom cabinet, chrome heated towel rail, double glazed window, marble half tiled wall and marble tiled floor.

Bedroom 5/Study

Ideal as an office or a nursery. Skylight and additional low level skylight with blinds, two large storage cupboards and additional under eave storage space. (Reduced ceiling height as room is in the eaves).

Rear Garden

49' South West facing secluded rear garden with a mature large willow tree. Flowerbed and shrubs borders with central lawn. Raised patio with steps down to the garden, water tap, fenced borders, gate to the rear.

Tenure

Freehold.

Local Authority

London Borough of Wandsworth.

Directions

From Southfields tube station turn left down Wimbledon Park Road. Second left into Pulborough Road and at the end turn right onto Granville Road. The house is just in on the right hand side before St Michael's School.
