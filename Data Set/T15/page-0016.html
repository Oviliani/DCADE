World Immunopathology Organization : IV World Asthma & COPD Forum. 30/04/2011 - 03/05/2011
Description: The lectures and discussions during this Congress will cover every aspect of basic research in the field of asthma, COPD and respiratory allergy, and will also bring forth the latest innovations in treatment. The prevalence of asthma and COPD around the world, their severity and mortality rates allow us to consider them as the most socially significant diseases of today and the nearest future. Our invitation especially addresses specialists in allergology, immunology, pulmonology, pediatrics, dermatology, critical care medicine etc. The Congress Program will cover a wide variety of areas—from mechanisms and genetic aspects of asthma and COPD to new advances in diagnostics, treatment and prophylaxis.
