INTERNATIONAL
International Council of Nurses : ICN Conference and CNR. 02/05/2011 - 08/05/2011
Description: This ICN International Conference will present strong evidence demonstrating how nurses, as key members of the health services team, promote and contribute to quality and accessible health care. The Conference will provide opportunities to disseminate nursing knowledge and leadership across specialities, cultures and countries. The three ICN pillars – Professional practice, Regulation and Socio-economic Welfare – will frame the scientific programme and the dynamic exchange of experiences and expertise.

The main objectives of the Conference are:
* To advance and improve the coverage and quality of health services
* To demonstrate the nursing contribution to the health of individuals, families and communities
* To provide opportunities for an in-depth exchange of experience and expertise within and beyond the international nursing community.
