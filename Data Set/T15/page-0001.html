INTERNATIONAL
Kazan Federal University : Fundamental Medicine: From Scalpel toward Genome, Proteome and Lipidome. 22/04/2011 - 28/04/2011
Description: Kazan Federal university

In collaboration with: Russian Academy of Sciences
Russian Academy of Medical Sciences
Academy of Sciences of Republic of Tatarstan
Science and Education Ministry of Republic of Tatarstan

Dedicated to the 50th Anniversary of 1961 Nobel Prize for DNA double helix structure

On Honor Presidentship of Ilshat R. Gafurov, the Rector of Kazan Federal University

Fundamental Medicine: From Scalpel toward Genome, Proteome and Lipidome

Internet 3D Conference on "Fundamental Medicine: From Scalpel toward Genome, Proteome and Lipidome" in virtual form, devoted to the 50th Anniversary of Nobel prize for DNA double helix structure will be held on 25-28 April 2011 in Kazan Federal university, Kazan, Russian Federation.

Topics / Symposia:
Genomic and predictive medicine
Gene diagnostics
Human and Medical genetics
Gene and Gene-cell therapy
Proteome and pathogenesis
Lipids in pathogenesis
Medical cybernetics
Biomedical informatics and ethics issues
Events
Symposia talks
Plenary lectures
Posters

Organizing Committee:

Honorary Chairman
James Watson (Cold Spring Harbor, USA)

Honorary Co-Chairmen
Roger Kornberg (Stanford, USA)
Ferid Murad (Houston, USA)

Chairman
Renad Ibragimovich Zhdanov (Kazan/Moskow)
Co-Chairmen
Valentin Viktorovich Vlasov (Novosibirsk)
Aleksandr Nikolaevich Grechkin (Kazan)


Morning, Day-time and Evening (for oversea participants) symposia sessions are scheduled. Plenary lecture – 30 min, symposia talk – 20 min, poster talk – 5-10 min. Language – English, Russian.

Deadline for registration is 22 th March 2011 and for sending an abstract - 29 th March 2011.

Registration fee for participation includes 1 A5 page of abstract, post transfer of the abstracts book, CD disk and depend on the abstracts volume.
