INTERNATIONAL
The University of Adelaide : International Society of Critical Health Psychology - 7th Biennial Conference. 18/04/2011 - 20/04/2011
Description: The ISCHP conference provides a vibrant opportunity for health psychologists and scholars from related disciplines to explore ongoing and emerging issues in critical theory and practice in relation to health and health care. Attendance is therefore welcomed from scholars in any discipline with a critical orientation to the field of health. The conference also particularly welcomes and encourages students and emerging researchers.
Contact: Organising Committee
