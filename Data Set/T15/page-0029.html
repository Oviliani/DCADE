Centre for Evidence Based Practice, Bergen University College : The National Network Conference in evidence based practice 2011. 28/04/2011 - 29/04/2011
Description: Powerful Knowledge Transfer in Practice

The challenge we face today is to introduce new knowledge into practice. It is assumed that national strategies are followed up by implementing evidence-based practice in health and education of health professionals.

This is an important contribution to improving patient safety and quality implementation of evidence. This conference will focus on how we can achieve knowledge transfer in practice. This conference will help to strengthen our national network.
