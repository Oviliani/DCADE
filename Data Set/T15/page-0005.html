Oncology Nursing Society : ONS 36th Annual Congress. 28/04/2011 - 01/05/2011
Description: Congress is the educational event for oncology nurses. No other conference is as in tune with the learning needs of cancer nurses. At Congress, you'll get the latest education and have a chance to connect to more than 4,000 of your nursing peers. You’ll gain new knowledge that will sharpen your skills and make you a better nurse. You’ll also share ideas and information with other oncology nurses and become part of the ONS community.
Contact: Oncology Nursing Society
Email: customer.service@ons.org
Venue: Boston Convention and Exhibition Center, Boston, Massachusetts
Fees: Between $225-$625 . Please see conference website for fees and registration information.
Website: http://www.ons.org/CNECentral/Conferences/Congress
