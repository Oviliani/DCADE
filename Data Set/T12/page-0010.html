Description The IFC is pleased to announce the upcoming 2011 Symposium!

Mark your calendars and pack your bags, for we are going to Phoenix! It is time to make your travel plans to attend the informational and social ferret event of the year at the Fiesta Resort and Conference Center in Tempe, AZ! Our newly reorganized team has obtained a wonderful room rate at a comfortable, relaxing and well located resort with numerous amenities and put together a panel of speakers - some new and some returning favorites - that are sure to make this some of your most pleasant days of the year! Not to mention that there will be the fun little extras that always make the trips special.
Approved AAVSB Race Provider

Returning as the MC is Joel Vanderbush. Speakers include:
Dr. Matti Kiupel - ECE and Vaccine Research
Dr. Robert Wagner - Adrenal Disease and Vaccine Research
Dr. Jerry Murray - First Aid Kit Workshop; ADV
Dr. Avery Bennett - The Most Common Ferret Surgeries
Bev Lowton - English Art of Ferreting
Barb Carlson - Nursing Sick Ferrets Back to Health at Home
Ailigh Vanderbush - Ferret Behavioral Problems

Topics:
Shelter Networking, Fundraising, Fostering
Cancer Treatment
Cardio problems
Ferret First Aid Kit - Sponsored by PetSmart Charities
Disaster Workshop - Sponsored by PetSmart Charities

Special Room Rates include two nights before and two after event.
Please reference International Ferret Conference for discounted rate.
http://www.fiestainnresort.com

Hotel rates are $75/night for a double room, $89/night for a triple and $99/night for a quad. Two HOT breakfasts are included in the price for the room so that will cover another meal. Hor d'oeuvres are served in the evening as well.

We have taken extra efforts to reduce costs as much as possible and are providing some additional workshops for both shelter operators and the general attendee. More information will follow and, as usual, discussion about the symposium and room mates will be available on the yahoo list at http://pets.groups.yahoo.com/group/ferret_symposium/. Speakers will be announced and updated on the IFC website at ferretcongress.org as they are confirmed.
Additional Information Cost: Registration fees are
$175 for 3 day regular attendee
$80 one day regular attendee. Limit 1 one day pass per person.
$40 for the optional speaker dinner. Discounts available for shelter operators or vet techs/vet students.
Registration includes all breaks and lunch on Friday and Saturday.
