Just steps from historic Tudor City Park, Grand Central Terminal and the United Nations complex, discover this pre-war and charming triple mint home with fabulous layout and southern exposure. The newly renovated apartment features a wall of windows, and both bedrooms have en suite bathrooms. High ceilings, walnut floors, brand new high end appliances and custom finishes make this a real value. "The Cloister" is an architectural gem just east of Second Avenue, offering 24 hour doorman, resident super, laundry room, bike room and storage. Pied-a-terres, guarantors, co-signers, parental buying and pets are permitted.
