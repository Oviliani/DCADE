1989 BMW 635CSi Motorsport Edition. Misano Red with black Nappa leather. 84,000 miles, FSH from BMW and BMW specialists Munich Legends and Prestige German Cars. Only 4 owners from new. Tax & MOT Sept 2011.

This is the rare Motorsport Edition (1 of only 188) in Misano Red with black Nappa leather interior to full Highline specification.

I purchased the car 8 years ago from BMW experts and 6-series specialists, Munich Legends. They carried out a £3000 refurbishment before I took the car and since then it has continued to be a pampered specimen. This is a well known car having appeared in Total BMW (July 2006) and belongs to the Chairman of the BMW Car Club 6-Series Register. The condition is good enough to enter concours events (3rd in Class BMW Northern concours, 2009) and mechanically fit enough to take you wherever you might want to go.

All the big ticket items have been done:
• Wings replaced 2008
• Coolant hoses replaced 15/10/2005
• A/C fan replaced 23/5/2006
• AFM replaced 20/10/2006
• Battery replaced 05/06/2009
• Rear subframe bushes replaced 11/12/2009
• Stainless Steel exhaust: 18/12/2009

The specification includes:
• Electric windows

• Electric sunroof
• A/C
• Full Nappa leather interior

The Motorsport Specification includes:
• Limited Slip Differential
• M-Tec steering wheel
• Electric sports seats with memory on the drivers
• Sports suspension

Wheels are BMW Style 32 – 8 x 17 front 9 x 17 rears with Goodyear F1 tyres. The original TRX wheels are available for concours purists.
