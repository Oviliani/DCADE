Driver - Truck Driver / Owner-Operator
More owner-operators are choosing Schneider National than ever before, making it easy to see why we are the nation's premiere transportation company. If you are a professional owner-operator who's looking for the advantages of being leased on with a big company but values the support to help you run your business your way, look no further. This opportunity features the strength of Schneider's extensive freight network and trailer fleet, providing for more drop and hooks, and 24/7 business support that can dramatically improve the success of your business.
  • Live in selceted areas of Alabama, Florida or Tennessee
  • Primary travel area is east of the Rockies, but potentially travel within 48 states, no New York City.
  • Depending on preference, typically out 2 weeks with 2-3 days off
  • Lease purchase options available
  • 92 CPM loaded plus accessorial pay, plus fuel surcharge on all miles
  • 125,000 to 145,000 annually
  • 70% drop & hook, average utilization 2500 miles per week

Qualifications

  • New owner-operators benefit from at least 1 year of driving experience
  • Owner-operators and fleet drivers must be 21 years old with valid driver's license, good driving record, & solid work history
  • Hazmat endorsement preferred
