Tech Mgr-Sys Admin & Support
Manages a function or unit within the technology environment. May manage associates and be responsible for hiring, termination, performance management. Supports systems through maintenance, modification, and problem resolution to support ongoing delivery of services and/or operations. Formulates/designs systems scope and objectives for the applications/development of a major project or multiple lesser projects. Responsible for instructing, directing, and checking the quality and timeliness of other systems professionals. Works on most difficult or complex systems. Provides training and technical assistance to more junior staff.
Responsibilities:
Provide day-to-day Unix operations support for various mission critical Global Markets applications. Manage Infrastructure projects including system and O.S upgrades, datacenter transition and consolidation. Provide technical troubleshooting and root cause analysis of infrastructure issues affecting these systems in production. Resolve production issues within the complex trading infrastructure environment within the Bank.Drive Infrastructure projects and operational stability initiatives including firmware and system upgrades, Contingency recovery exercises, managed service provider connectivity and datacenter consolidation. Perform service management functions within the enterprise including release, change, problem and incident management.
Create application specific monitoring and Job scheduling using Enterprise systems and tools.
Perform capacity analysis of servers for the various applications to obtain server utilization metrics.
Requirement to work as part of on-call rotation and for scheduled work that happens on weekends and outside of business hours
Participates in triage and PPR meetings on high impact events
Engages in change review meetings with BTG partners to ensure operational readiness for upcoming changes
Engages engineering teams for process and application improvement opportunities
Assists in the training of junior associates

Qualifications:

Required Skills:

Knowledge of Unix/Linux Operating systems family, deep understanding of networking protocols, name resolution principles, experience with Veritas Clustering technologies. Understanding of Storage Area Networks. Experience with ESX VMWare products.

Desired Skills:

Experience working with RedHat 4.x/5.x, Solaris 10, Windows, Veritas Cluster Server (VCS), VXFS, EMC SAN, SRDF, NAS, NIS, NFS, DNS, Samba, TCP/IP Networking, Perl/Python Scripting, Virtualization


Other Locations:
