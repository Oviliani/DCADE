AVP, Associate Administrative Manager (Hopewell, NJ/Charlotte, NC/Lincoln, RI/Jax, FL)
The Associate Administrative Manager position will serve to ensure regulatory compliance and corporate mandates for all administrative and operational activities performed in the Merrill Edge Self Directed platform. A predominate objective of the position is to limit risk and financial exposure to the business unit and the firm.
Responsibilities include (but are not limited to) the following:
  • New Account Approval
  • Conduct supervisory reviews including RMS, EOD & OTR.
  • Cash to Margin review
  • Approving market action trade corrections and review/audit monthly error logs
  • Review and maintain all Compliance and Regulatory Correspondence including email and Fax2mail
  • Monitor accounts with large debit balances and short market values over $500,000 and coordinate approval with Credit Administration and clients

Qualifications:
  • Series 7, 63/65 or 66, 8 or 9/10 (or FINRA equivalent 4,24,53) required to be considered for the role
  • Strong Leadership and Communication skills
  • Merrill Edge self directed, MEAC or Branch Office CA, FA or Operations experience
  • Familiarity with compliance and regulatory issues
