Project Leader - Dover, NH
About Liberty Mutual Group "Helping people live safer, more secure lives" since 1912, Boston-based Liberty Mutual Group is a diversified global insurer and third largest property and casualty insurer in the U.S. based on A.M. Best Company's report of 2010 net written premium. The Group also ranks 82nd on the Fortune 100 list of largest corporations in the U.S. based on 2010 revenue. As of December 31, 2010, Liberty Mutual Group had $112.4 billion in consolidated assets, $95.4 billion in consolidated liabilities, and $33.2 billion in annual consolidated revenue. Liberty Mutual Group offers a wide range of insurance products and services, including personal automobile, homeowners, workers compensation, property, commercial automobile, general liability, global specialty, group disability, reinsurance and surety. Liberty Mutual Group (www.libertymutualgroup.com) employs over 45,000 people in more than 900 offices throughout the world.

Advance your career at Liberty Mutual - A Fortune 100 Company!
As the Project Leader you will serve as the business owner of Life company Architecture and agent portal and be responsible for leading the development and implementation of a highly visible state-of-the-art portal. You will be a hands-on business leader tasked with developing and progressing online suite of tools provided to the sales staff and the customers by establishing more robust digital capabilities to accelerate customer acquisition, broaden the Company's online capabilities and deepen customer relationships. The Project Leader manages all aspects of complex Departmental or Division projects. Negotiates project objectives and direction with senior managers. Projects vary in scope and duration
Responsibilities:
* Implement business vision and roadmap for Individual Life Architecture and agent portal capabilities.
* Works with technology project managers on business requirements, functional design, prototyping, process design (including scenario design, flow mapping), testing, training, defining support procedures and supporting implementations for an integrated portal platform; oversees implementation and manages continual system improvements.
* Translates Web Department/Division needs into a comprehensive project plan which ensures successful project completion.
* Conceptualizes immediate and long-range analytical needs of the lines of business. Must understand business objectives as they relate to project goals.
* Negotiates analytics projects objectives and direction with SBU heads, Division Senior Vice Presidents and Managers of such departments as Information Systems, Corporate Research, Actuarial, Underwriting, Claims, Sales, Legal, and Human Resources.
* Negotiates with external agencies/organizations such as vendors and State insurance departments regarding sensitive and/or complex issues.
* Builds solid understanding of existing data sources and limitations. Identifies sources and relationships of corporate data and makes recommendations / decisions regarding consolidation and storage of data for access by Senior Managers.
* Coaches and develops direct reports to be efficient and independent in handling small to medium ad-hoc data / analysis projects.
* Manages and allocates individual contributor time to effectively support other analytical projects within the team.
* Ensures continuous communication with other Project Managers and the Unified Project Manager to identify dependencies and manage support needs.
* Performs comprehensive data analysis and builds moderately complex models to analyze and explain trends and demonstrate impact from process initiatives. Manages assigned individual contributors to ensure the completion of work in a timely and efficient manner.
* Presents analysis findings and recommendations to Senior Management and Product Line Managers as necessary.



Requirements:

* Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience, advanced degree or professional designation preferred.
* Minimum of 7-9 years progressively more responsible experience in a financial and/or project. management environment with exposure to project planning, simulation, modeling, capital budgeting, cost benefit analysis and information systems development required.
* Strong negotiation skills also required.
* Minimum of 3 years of management experience required.
