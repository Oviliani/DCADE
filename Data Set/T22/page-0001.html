Program/Project Manager
A Program/Project Manager is responsible for the full end-to-end delivery of business solutions to the business. A Program/Project Manager is a hands-on expert in project planning and execution including: scope definition and managements, task and resource scheduling, cost and price budgeting, quality review and assurance, client relationship management, project team management, and overall change management. The Program/Project Manager will be 100% accountable for the on-time, on budget delivery of solutions per the approved specifications.

This resource will manage all phases of the Software Development Lifecycle, from initial ideation phases to the final project closure activities. To be successful, this resource must be detailed oriented and willing to dive into the details of a project to flush out issues and risks, while simultaneously possessing the ability to see the bigger picture and understand the interdependencies of the overall program. A Program/Project Manager will be responsible for large complex projects that span organizations, geographies, and technologies, often with direct accountability to Executive Management. This resource must have excellent leadership skills and must be able to work with a diverse project team consisting of resources from all parts of the organization (both IT & Business).

The Program/Project Manager will likely be responsible for either a) a portfolio of projects that together achieve a common business goal and objective, or b) a single large project or program that consists of multiple phases of both IT and Business components.

The Program/Project Manager will be a strategic thinking, quality driven individual, who will provide leadership, direction, coordination and coaching for project managers and project teams during their day to day management and implementation of IT & Business projects, programs, activities and processes.


Requirements:

Maintains overall accountability for the successful delivery of the designated project / program to the Business.

Establishes work plan tracking procedures, status reporting, issue management, change management, resource management, configuration management, financial management, etc.

Coordinates, directs and monitors IT & Business programs ensuring consistency of processes across projects

Communicates with program/project managers and solution managers to ensure projects adhere to all phases of software development lifecycle management

Ensures that all ongoing projects are run within the constraints of scope, quality, time and cost

Conducts and supports training programs for the program/project managers and teams

Creates reports and documentation to measure status of projects and review results on a weekly, monthly and quarterly basis

Manages 3rd party vendors selected to participate in assigned projects, including full vendor management procedures.

Performs analysis of program/project issues, resolves problems, identifies and recommends improvement opportunities as related to the policies of the PMO. Participates in PMO functions, processes, and rollout.

Facilitates the detailed definition and scoping of projects including both the IT portions of a project and the Business / Business process components of the project.

Prepares estimates and develops project plans.

Coordinates with both Business Unit resources as well as IT resources.

Manages communication between internal customers and various functional groups

Provides technical consultation to other IT groups.

Participates in vendor evaluations and provides recommendations.

Improves quality results by implementing unit, system and integration testing cycles.

Understands and uses critical path management.

Proactively identifies and communicates risks to project development and schedule.

Monitors and drives resolution of issues.

Adheres to clients Governance, PM methods, and tools/templates as appropriate.

- Prior Tier 1 Consulting Experience preferred
- 12+ years of total work experience preferred
- 7+ years of PM experience required
- 2+ years of PMO experience
