Wisconsin Dells Destination Showcase

Starts: Tuesday June 07, 2011 at 11:30AM CDT
Ends: Tuesday June 07, 2011 at 02:00PM CDT
Event Type: Other
Region: Madison, Wisconsin Area

Price:

The Wisconsin Dells Visitor & Convention Bureau is excited to announce the 2011 Destination Showcase for Meeting & Event Planners.

Please join the Wisconsin Dells Visitor & Convention Bureau at our Destination Showcase featuring Wisconsin Dells area convention resorts, hotels, and restaurants. Enjoy a complimentary buffet lunch, network, and register for prizes such as overnight stays, golf, waterpark passes, and a $250 Wisconsin Dells gift card.

Drop in any time between 11:30 AM and 2:00 PM. Lunch will be served from 11:30 – 2:00 PM. Prize drawings will be held at 12:30 PM, 1:15 PM, and 2:00 PM.

RSVP by contacting Lindsey Parker, lindsey@wisdells.com or 608/254-7180 ext 313. Deadline to register is June 1, 2011. You are welcome to invite other meeting or event professionals from your organization.
