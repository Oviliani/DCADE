Yankee Group announces that 4G World 2010, held Oct. 18-21 at McCormick Place in Chicago, attracted over 10,000 registered attendees and more than 250 sponsors, including 150 exhibitors. The company also announced its 2011 event schedule, including 4G World China 2011, which will be hosted by T&S International Commerce & Cultural Development Center in Chengdu on April 27-29, 2011, and 4G World 2011, which will be held Oct. 24-27, 2011, at McCormick Place in Chicago. The event Web site is http:/www.4GWorld.com. More information will be added soon. Meanwhile, contact Kate Walsh to sponsor, exhibit, or present.
