AHRI National Convention 2011

Starts: Tuesday June 07, 2011 at 07:30AM EST
Ends: Wednesday June 08, 2011 at 05:00PM EST
Event Type: Tradeshow/Convention

Darling Harbour

Price: See conference website for details

Industry: human resources
Keywords: Ahri, Hr, National Convention, Conference, Human Resources
Intended For: CEO, HR Manager, Manager
Organization: Australian Human Resources Institute (AHRI)

The AHRI National Convention is the biggest HR event in Australia. Each year, it attracts over 2,000 participants and it’s here that you will meet leading HR practitioners and listen to presentations from some of the greatest minds in business and HR from Australia and around the world.
