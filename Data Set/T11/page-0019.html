3rd Annual Cloud Computing World Forum

Starts: Tuesday June 21, 2011 at 08:00AM PDT
Ends: Wednesday June 22, 2011 at 05:00PM PDT
Event Type: Conference
Region: Harrow, United Kingdom
Location: Olympia
Earls Court
London, GREATER LONDON GB
Price: http://www.cloudcomputinglive.com/worldforum/register.html
Website: http://www.cloudwf.com
Industry: computer software
Keywords: Computing, Conference, Exhibition, Expo, Forum, Technology, Show, Cloud, It, Saas
Intended For: * CIO/CTOs/CxOs * Directors * Strategy Directors * Communication Directors * Heads of Marketing * Heads of Business Development * Heads of Sales * Heads of Call and Contact Managers * Product Managers * Bus
Organization: Cloud Computing World Series

Taking place on the 21st and 22nd June 2011 at Olympia, London, the 3rd Annual Cloud Computing World Forum promises to be Europe’s largest Cloud and SaaS based event.

Building on the success of the 2010 show, this two day conference offers the most complete and comprehensive platform for the global Cloud Computing and SaaS industry.

The forum includes a conference and free-to-attend exhibition, providing a full perspective of cloud computing and SaaS from business value through integration and implementation, to the future of business computing.

Further complimentary streams and seminars available at the show will include Virtualization, Mobile Computing, Enterprise 2.0, Green IT and Enterprise Apps.

Visit www.cloudcomputinglive.com/worldforum for more information.
