Join Endurance as Inside Sales Rep NOW and build a career with one of the Nation’s premier providers of Vehicle Service Contracts!

Job Description

We are currently looking to fill several positions for Certified Vehicle Protection Specialists. These specialists are aggressive self starters with great attitudes that help serve our clients and grow our business through selling our vehicle service contracts. Making the client happy is always the first priority in our office. Unparalleled sales and customer service skills are natural gifts required to be a part of our team. This is an ideal opportunity for individuals with a strong desire to work in a vigorous inside sales arena. This is more than a job; this is a chance for a career with a company as unique, fresh, and high-powered as you.

This position involves no telemarketing or cold calling; all calls are inbound. The average Inside Sales Rep makes $65k+ with our top performers making $100K+! There is unlimited earning potential as this is a heavily commission based position (with some base).

Our training has helped many entry-level Sales Reps become successful in the sales industry. We have also accepted many recent college graduates, helping them grow just as our company has. Our top performers all have the following skills and abilities:
• Open to making contacts over the phone and through networking
• Ability to relate to all types of personalities
• Driven and goal-oriented, focused on success
• Ability to work independently
• Excellent time management and organizational skills
• Good communication skills
• High energy
• Use of good judgment


The hours we are currently hiring for are 11:00am – 8:00pm Monday – Friday; half days every other Saturday.

We have limited openings that will be filled as soon as possible.
Endurance Offers You:
• Unlimited COMMISSION (+ Base)
• Top-Notch paid training
• Excellent advancement and growth opportunities
• Unlimited earning potential
o First year expected earnings of $65,000 with the ability to make much more
o Our top performers earn over $100,000/year
• Health benefits Package Available
• Casual and friendly work environment
