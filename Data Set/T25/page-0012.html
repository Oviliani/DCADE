At the earliest date possible we are looking for Assistant Child and Adolescent Psychiatrists.

Job Description

At the earliest date possible we are looking for

Assistant Child and Adolescent Psychiatrists

who can imagine working in one of our dedicated teams with a high degree of individual responsibility and lots of scope for design in a pleasant working atmosphere, which is characterised by directness and esteem. This position is open for graduates.

Our offer: We provide…

  • a responsible, varied and permanent position
  • salary based on the state employees salary scheme “TV-Ärzte” and retirement provision
  • relocation help
  • flexible working hours, overtime compensation through time ‘in lieu’
  • professional guidance, regular external team supervision
  • the Head Child and Adolescent Psychiatrist is fully qualified for the supervision of assistant Child and Adolescent Psychiatrists and for behavioural therapy
  • payment of all costs regarding further psychotherapeutic training

Your competences: You…

  • are a sociable, open-minded, well-structured personality
  • are a team player and interested in cooperation with fellow professionals
  • have a basic working-knowledge of written and spoken German
  • have empathy for our patients and their families
  • can cultivate a result-oriented and issue-driven way of working
  • can keep steady and calm even in difficult situations
  • are interested in distinguished diagnostic techniques and therapeutic methods

Marsberg is situated in the attractive region of the eastern Sauerland, is family-friendly and offers all necessary opportunities for school education and recreational activities. There is a good road and rail connection to the nearby towns of Paderborn and Kassel, where private location is also possible.

In cases of equal qualification,aptitude and expertise of the applicants, female applicants will be given preferential treatment, unless there are preponderant reasons to give preference to another applicant (§ 8 Article 6 of the North Rhine-Westphalian Equal Opportunities Act Landesgleichstellungsgesetz NW). Applications from severely disabled people with appropriate suitability are explicitly welcome.

We have awoken your interest? For further information, please contact Dr. med. Falk Burchard (+49-2992-601-3101 /falk.burchard@wkp-lwl.org) or find out more on www.jugendpsychiatrie-marsberg.de.
