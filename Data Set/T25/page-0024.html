Job Description

Traffic Supply Chain Coordinator

COMPANY PROFILE:

Belvika Trade & Packaging Ltd. is a one-stop packaging company, specializing in precision packing of dry foods, chocolate and confectionary products. They are currently located in Mississauga, Ontario.

BENEFITS:

  • Full time position
  • Salary: To be negotiated
  • Air conditioned facility

RESPONSIBILITIES include, but not limited to:

Belvika is looking for a Traffic Supply Chain Coordinator to manage the day to day coordinating and supervision of planned shipment traffic. We are looking for a professional candidate who has a lot of energy who is looking to join a fun company.

  • Lead, manage and ensure shipping processes and procedures are followed by internal personnel
  • Ensure all shipments to customers are in compliance with each customers compliance and routing requirements
  • Monitor customer routing and compliance guides weekly to ensure customer requirements are met
  • Communicate and document all routing and compliance requirements or changes in requirements to appropriate internal personnel and 3PL contacts
  • Monitor and implement continuous improvement initiatives to advance service levels to customers, profitability of company, and other employees
  • Maintain accurate shipping records, forms and related documentation for both internal and external requirements
  • Responsible for coordination with trucking companies for pick ups and delivery
  • Ensure accurate inbound and outbound counts
  • Communicate pick up and/or deliveries with customers
  • Including all other duties assigned and required by Production Planning Manager or Production Plant Manager

REQUIREMENTS:

  • Must be fluent in English reading and writing
  • Previous order entry experience
  • Previous Traffic/Supply chain experience
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
  • Minimum 2 - 5 years experience
  • Excellent leadership and communication skills
  • Computer Skills: Previous ERP or MRP System Experience examples SAP, Epicor, Dymnamics, Sage, Pack Manager
  • MS Office and understanding of Network Server environment

For immediate consideration, please email your resume to: belvika@humanresources101.com
