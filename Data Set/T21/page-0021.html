Job Description

The Department of Ophthalmology at UT Southwestern Medical Center announces an opening for an Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, or Professor. This position will serve to help supervise residents and fellows at Parkland Memorial Hospital. Duties will include primary oversight of ophthalmology consultation and hospital/emergency department patients; supervision of new and general ophthalmology clinics; and supervision of procedures in operating rooms, minor surgery rooms, and laser suites. In addition to general teaching duties for residents and fellows, opportunity exists for participation in faculty practice (either as comprehensive ophthalmologist or in applicant’s area of subspecialty training).

Interested individuals should send curriculum vitae and a cover letter to:
 Preston Blomquist, M.D.
Associate Professor
Residency Program Director
Department of Ophthalmology
UT Southwestern Medical Center
5323 Harry Hines Blvd.
Dallas, TX 75390-9057

UT Southwestern Medical Center at Dallas is an Affirmative Action/Equal Opportunity Employer

Job Requirements

Applicant must have MD degree, must be board certified or board eligible in ophthalmology with a Texas Medical License.
