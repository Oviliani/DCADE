Description

Located in one of Brooklyn's most energetic enclaves Carroll Gardens West, this new building is close to not only Brooklyn's waterfront but some of the best restaurant's such as Good Fork, Lucali's and Alma. Each of the 10 homes are accessed through a private elevator entrance and when the doors open you're going to blown away by the incredible light that fills the apartments. The rear homes which face Manhattan offer jaw dropping views of lower Manhattan from every room. High ceilings and oversized windows add to the dramatic appeal to every home in the building. The open kitchens offer a gorgeous Kitchenaid appliance package, granite countertops and an island for casual dining. Central air conditioning, private master baths and select homes with private outdoor space make 49 Woodhull easy to call home. Less
