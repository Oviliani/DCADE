Description

Two houses on one lot: Main house: 2 bedrooms, 1 bath Rear cottage: 1 bedroom, 1 bath Located on a quintessential hillside street in one of San Francisco`s most coveted neighborhoods, this rare property offers a double treat: A serene Victorian home PLUS a detached two-level cottage in the rear! Use both as a family compound or live in one and rent the other ? either way, this gorgeous property offers great flexibility and tremendous value. The main house retains its beautiful original details but has been thoughtfully modernized with wide-open spaces and contemporary finishes. The living and dining rooms flow together as one and feature a gas fireplace, newer dual-pane windows and George Nelson lighting. The large kitchen features ample cabinetry, slate flooring, stainless appliances, and concealed laundry, and opens to a wonderful family room, which steps down to the sunny rear deck. The brand new bath is a treat for the senses, with gleaming shower over deep soaking tub, subway tile with handsome glass accents, a floating vanity, lighted magnifying mirror, and plentiful storage. Both of the bright bedrooms have newer dual-glazed windows and Nelson lighting, while the entry hall closet reveals a brilliant surprise: a great home office nook! The home also features a huge undeveloped attic with three skylights and a height of approximately 10 feet at the peak, while the large basement offers additional expansion potential. The rear cottage offers an updated full kitchen, dining area and laundry on the lower level, and a large living room, bedroom and remodeled bath upstairs, with fantastic east bay and downtown views. Because it`s fully detached, the cottage is the perfect rental unit, or a great family space or au pair/in-law apartment. There is two-car parking in the rear, accessed by Nellie Alley. This property grants all the big wishes ? location, charm, and value ? times two! Please visit: www.22ndStreetCottages.com Less
