PERSONAL:
  • Yarbrough signed with Cardinals scout Randy Benson on July 11, 2003 after the club drafted him in the 5th round in June, 2003.
  • Before 2005 spring training, the Baseball America Prospect Handbook rated Brandon as 13th-best prospect in the Cardinals organization.

    TRANSACTIONS
  • December, 2009: Yarbrough signed with the Reds organization.
  • Janaury 12, 2011: Brandon signed with the Rockies organization.
BATTING:
  • Yarbrough is a good lefthanded hitter. He has a short, balanced swing with very good bat speed. He should add more power as he matures as a hitter.
  • Brandon hits the ball to all fields. And he is willing to take the ball back up the middle. He is also willing to take a base on balls. But he needs to make contact more consistently.
FIELDING:
  • Yarbrough is working to improve his receiving skills. He needs to improve his ability to block pitches in the dirt, too. Better footwork should take care of that.
  • Brandon has a slightly-above-average arm, but needs to get better on his transfer and release.
  • In 2004, Yarbrough threw out 32 percent of those trying to steal a base.
