PERSONAL:
  • Lawrence played in the Yankees organization in 2003. But his agent and mets assistant general manager Kevin Morgan knew each other from college, and during 2004 spring training Horace switched training camps from Tampa to Port St. Lucie.
  • Horace credits Mets minor league Managers Mookie Wilson and Brett Butler with their instruction.

    "They taught me so much about this gme that I didn't already know before," he said. "The little things in between the lines as far as playing outfield, plus the mental aspect that I didn't have before I got here."
  • Lawrence, a product of the University of South Dakota, has a degree in psychology. He started graduate school in September, 2004.
