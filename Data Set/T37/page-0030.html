PERSONAL:
  • Hodge is a solid, organizational-type player.
  • In 2002, Kevin made the rather amazing tranisition from "utility guy" to relief pitcher! And he did it well, going 3-2, 2.97 with six saves, eight walks and 28 strikeouts in 30 innings for New Britain. At Fort Myers he went 3-0, 0.61 in 14 innings with three saves, three walks and 18 strikeouts.
  • November, 2004: Hodge signed with the Astros organization.
  • June 16, 2005: The Phillies acquired Hodge from the Astros to complete an earlier trade (on April 21).
