PERSONAL:
  • Christian was signed by Red Sox scout Miguel Garcia in 2002.
  • Lara was named the Red Sox' player of the year in the Dominican Summer League in 2003, his pro debut.
  • During the off-season before 2005 spring training, Baseball America rated Lara as 12th-best prospect in the Red Sox organization. And in January, 2006, they had Christian as #18 in the Boston farm system.
  • April 7, 2008: The Dodgers sent RHP Eric Hull to the Red Sox, acquiring Lara and cash.
BATTING:
  • Lara exhibits excellent patience and excellent bat control at the plate. He works his way on via a walk, a bunt or a base hit. His bat-handling skills are very good.
  • A switch-hitter, Christian endeavors to make solid contact from both sides of the plate. He will hit a lot of doubles, but should only develop into a guy capable of 10 or 15 home runs in a good year.
  • Lara is an intelligent player and understands what he needs to do to improve. So he should eventually maximize his ability.
FIELDING:
  • Christian has very good hands and body control, allowing him to make all the plays at shortstop. (Baseball America-9/20/04)
  • Lara is a slick-fielding shortstop who can make the very difficult play and make it look easy. He has a fluidity in his play, showing solid instincts and an ability to get to balls others can't reach. He has good body control and understands the game very well.
  • His mechanics and actions in the field are those of a more veteran shortstop. But he is still prone to some throwing errors.
  • In 2005, Christian led the South Atlantic League with a .951 fielding pct.
RUNNING:
  • Christian is very speedy.
