PERSONAL:
  • McCarthy signed with Braves scout J.J. Picollo after the team chose him in the 6th round in 2001.
  • Before 2005 spring training, the Baseball America Prospect Handbook had McCarthy as the 21st-best prospect in the Braves farm system.
  • Billy exhibits excellent makeup.
  • June, 2007: The Royals sent 1B Graham Koonce to the Braves, acquiring McCarthy.
BATTING:
  • McCarthy has good pop in his bat and hits to all fields by driving pitches while standing close to the plate to cover the entire strike zone.
FIELDING:
  • McCarthy displays real good range and a strong arm -- enough for right field. But overall, Billy plays best in left field.
RUNNING:
  • Billy is a smart baserunner, but isn't super fast.
CAREER INJURY REPORT:
  • April 14-May 1, 2005: McCarthy was on the D.L. with an injured ankle. Early in July, he received a cortisone shot for plantar fascitis in his heel.
