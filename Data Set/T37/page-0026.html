PERSONAL:
  • Orlando is the son of former Major League catcher Orlando Mercado. And Junior may have better overall skills than his dad.
  • During the winter before 2010 spring training, Mercado posted a .337 average with 12 RBI in 28 games with Carolina in the Puerto Rican Winter League.

    TRANSACTIONS
  • December 18, 2009: Mercado signed with the Braves organization.
  • January, 2011: Orlando signed with the Dodgers organization.
BATTING:
  • Mercado should be a better hitter than his father. Orlando Junior makes real good contact and has an excellent eye, drawing more walks than strikeouts.
  • Orlando has power to both gaps.
FIELDING:
  • Mercado is developing into an excellent defensive catcher.
  • Orlando has an average arm as far as strength, but his quick release makes him above average in throwing out attempting base-thieves.
  • He calls a good game and is already a solid receiver.
CAREER INJURY REPORT:
  • Mid-June, 2010: Mercado was on the D.L. for about two weeks.
