2011 International Conference on Technological Advancements in Civil Engineering (ICTACE 2011) is the premier forum for the presentation of new advances and research results in the fields of theoretical, experimental, and applied Technological Advancements in Civil Engineering . The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world.
