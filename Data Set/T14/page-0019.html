2011 2nd International Conference ON Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Technologies (MIMT 2011) is the premier forum for the presentation of new advances and research results in the fields of Mechanical, Industrial, and Manufacturing Technologies.  
 
The conference will bring together leading researchers, engineers and scientists in the domain of interest from around the world. The Conference proceeding will be published by IEEE Press. All the accepted papers will be indexed by the major indexing services, such as INSPEC, and EI (Compendex).
