Title: 2011 International Conference on System Modeling and Optimization
StartDate: 01/26/2011
EndDate: 01/28/2011
Location guiyang china asia 
URL: http://www.icsmo.org/


ICSMO 2011, aims to bring together researchers, scientists, engineers, and scholar students to exchange and share their experiences, new ideas, and research results about all aspects of System Modeling and Optimization, and discuss the practical challenges encountered and the solutions adopted. 
 
The conference will be held every year to make it an ideal platform for people to share views and experiences in Information and Education Technology and related areas. The ICSMO 2011 proceedings will be published by IEEE and All the papers will be archived in the IEEE Xplore and indexed by Ei Compendex. 
 
English is the official language of the conference. We welcome paper submissions. Prospective authors are invited to submit full (and original research) papers (which is NOT submitted/published/under consideration anywhere in other conferences/journal) in electronic (PDF only) format through the easy chair conferences management system website or via email icsmo@iacsit.org.
