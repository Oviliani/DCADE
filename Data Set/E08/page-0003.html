Synopsis

George Clooney, Mark Wahlberg and Ice Cube lead a ragtag band of Gulf War grunts itchin' to grab millions in gold from Saddam Hussein. Along the way, they discover more action than they could ever want and, surprisingly, their humanity.

See movie reviews below.

About the DVD: The Three Kings DVD is especially praiseworthy, demonstrating the astonishing degree of craft and care that went into creating the film. Not only are you given a tour of the sets, for example, but you also get CNN footage of the Gulf War. The image is in 2.35:1 anamorphic widescreen, and the sound is in Dolby Digital 5.1. Extras include two commentaries from Russell and the producers; featurettes; video diaries; deleted scenes; production stills; and a funny, razor-sharp dissection of the surreal universe of modern filmmaking from Jonze, the director of Being John Malkovich.
