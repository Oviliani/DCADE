Director Michael Bay gives FDR's "date that will live in infamy" epic scope. Two daring young pilots, Rafe McCawley (Ben Affleck) and Danny Walker (Josh Hartnett) vie for the affections of comely Army nurse Evelyn Stewart (Kate Beckinsale). But their love has only just begun to bloom when their personal destinies -- and that of a world on the brink of war -- intervene.

The will romance that will tug your heartstrings and the combat sequences are stunning; the attack on December 7th - an impressive mix of live action and CGI will have you battening down the hatches.
